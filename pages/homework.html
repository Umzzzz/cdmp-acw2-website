Surrealism art movement is an unusual piece of art. At the time, there was a lot of killing going on during the post war. Therefore, the surrealist artist wanted a way to show their emotion through their piece of work and give the humanity some sort of hope for the future of living. Surrealist iconic artist like Salvador Dali, wanted to set people free from the misery from what happen in the war. So he showed his emotion through is art by applying modernism, which gave people hope of the future.

Therefore, I thought I give a go with my version of surrealism. I chose to do a piece of art on myself. Where I am merging two images to make a cool effect in Photoshop in this Digital era. The art piece consist of me in a suit a self-portrait and overlay layer image of the amazing building of Emporio Armani in Manchester. The reason behind this piece of art was I wanted to show that I in a suit and big building show some sort of my future career in the industry, working in some sort of office. I could see myself working in a nice architecture building like the Emporio Armani. In addition, it shows that I dream of expensive lifestyle in future, wearing top designer clothing like Emporio Armani.
